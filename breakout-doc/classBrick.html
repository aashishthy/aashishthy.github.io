Note: Part1 specifies that we need to have a total of 4 rows with 12 bricks in each row. So the width, height, x and y co-ordinate of each of the bricks should be a function of SCREEN_WIDTH and SCREEN_HEIGHT (Assuming all bricks are adjacent are placed adjacent to each other)

The x and y co-ordinates of the bricks should be adjusted so that bricks are placed side by side

Example: Co-ordinates of row 0 bricks:(0, 0), (10, 0), (20, 0)....(110,0) Co-ordinates of row 1 bricks:(0, 10), (10, 10), (20, 10)....(110, 10) Co-ordinates of row 2 bricks:(0, 20), (10, 20), (20, 20)....
